Current Workshops

We are always hosting new upcycling project workshops! We unveil a new round of workshops every month to keep up with the latest trends in upcycled fashion.

  • Workshop Rates
  • Drop-In: $10.00 CAD
  • 4 Class Pass: $35.00 CAD

Upycling Project: Pop Can Tab Tote

Weave together a flashy tote made entirely of pop can tabs! All supplies are provided.


Upycling Project: 100 Uses for a Safety Pin

This handy little device does it all. Join us and witness exactly how this seemingly mundane object will change your life! All supplies are provided.


Upycling Project: Natural Charm Bracelets

An ode to the beauty of nature! This project involves transforming flora into charm bracelets. All supplies are provided, but participants are encouraged to bring custom pieces.


Project: Hemp Weaving

Technically speaking this class does not involve upcycling, however, but we couldn't resist including it in the March 2014 line-up. The wonders of hemp are myriad. Join this workshop and learn the art of hemp weaving. Create bracelets, necklaces, rings, and more with this sturdy natural fibre.
